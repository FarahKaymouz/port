Theater Design: Rust-oration

This design is an ode to rust and the stage's metamorphosis. Drawing from performers' movements, it weaves geometry in an additive dance, where spaces become intimate, devoid of conventional thresholds. Like nature's embrace of the artificial, I've melded organic and angular forms, echoing the hues of rust. Through the rust to burgundy palette, the atmosphere harmonizes, engaging with theatrical lights, inviting the audience into an immersive journey beyond the performance.

FemmeFusionFoundry

FFF - A makerspace-gallery celebrating the essence of womanhood through diverse cultural influences and interdisciplinary practices, built after the research of one multidisciplinary artist. It fosters creativity, heritage, and community under the umbrella of female craftsmanship. The womanhood voice is translated in a language of plaster and mesh, intertwining strength and fluidity, and all that is in between.
